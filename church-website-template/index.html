About Us

This is who we are.

While we are first and foremost a New Testament Church, with our main priority on worshipping our heavenly Father, we are much more than that. We are a family of believers knit together by our common love and commitment to Jesus Christ; a family that desires to see one another become increasingly grounded in Christ through the teaching of practical, Biblical truth; and a group of believers who want to see one another cultivate and use their spiritual gifts to serve and honor the Lord. We hope you will join us to experience solid Bible teaching and preaching; godly, uplifting music; and an atmosphere of loving fellowship in Christ. We would love for you to find a place to grow with us as we gather to worship, pray, and connect with God and each other.

The purpose of the Victory Baptist Bible Church shall be:

  1. To worship and serve the God of the Bible in spirit and in truth, in agreement with Biblical revelation enlightened by the Holy Spirit, and in accordance with our God given conscience.
  2. To love, strengthen and provoke one another unto good works by the mutual edification of one another, utilizing those spiritual gifts given to each member by the Holy Spirit.
  3. To fulfill the great commission of the Lord Jesus Christ by the proclamation of the message of salvation through personal evangelism and missionary endeavors, as well as to disciple those who accept Jesus Christ as their personal Saviour (Matthew 28:18-20).
  4. To observe the ordinances given to the church by Jesus Christ, which are baptism and the Lord’s Table.
  5. To endeavor to establish other local New Testament churches in this country and others.
